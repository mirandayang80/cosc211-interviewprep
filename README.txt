Final Project: Interview Prep

# of command line arguments: 0

Run either "Project.java" (array stack version) or "ProjectLL.java" (doubly linked list version)

Home screen:
* Select 1 category of interview questions to practice. "All" combines both behavioral and quantitative questions

Second screen:
* Type your answer to the question using your keyboard (no keep to click on the text box)
* Click next to get another randomly selected interview question. Your answer will be saved
* Click "Review Answers" button to review the list of questions you answerwd
* Click "Home" to go back to the home screen

Review Answers screen:
* Click the up arrow to see further down the list (questions you answered will be moved up one slot each time you click)
* Click the down arrow to see further up the list (questions you answered will be moved down one slot each time you click)
* Click "Home" to go back to the home screen
